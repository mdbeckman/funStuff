  • Ryan Jack Allred · Web Designer at B2d Marketing
    I love my bike! I've been carless for about four years in Seattle and I save so much money.

    Yearly car cost = $1200 insurance + $960 parking ($80/month) + $60-120 tabs/emissions + $720-1000 in gas (or more), totaling at least $3000 / year. This is a rough estimate on the low end and doesn't even include repairs, tickets, tolls, etc..

    Bike cost over 4 years = $450 for a used bike + maybe $100 in maintenance per year, totaling about $212 / year. Oh, and I've lost 20 pounds.
